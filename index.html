About Scalpé

Where Medical Precision Meets Aesthetic Mastery

Scalpé Hair & Scalp Institute is a globally recognised centre of excellence for advanced hair restoration and scalp health. With clinics in Bangkok, London, and Dubai, our specialist medical team has transformed the lives of over 15,000 patients since 2012.

All treatments are performed exclusively by GMC-regulated specialist doctors, using only the most advanced evidence-based technologies available globally.
